Registration Guidelines

Follow the step by step instructions below to register. Registration can be done only through California Tamil Academy website.
Returning Students:
  1. Open the url, https://www.catamilacademy.org/cta/login.aspx and login with your user credentials
  2. Click on 'Child Registration' from the menu ‘Parent Access’
  3. a) Select student name from the drop down list box if registering son/daughter who is already a student or
    b) Click 'New Student Registration' if registering a sibling of an existing student
  4. Fill in all the details, marked *
  5. Select ‘Other’ for public school city/name and fill in the box on the right side
  6. Click Submit button and follow the wizard (incl. agreeing/signing to Rules & Regulations, Waiver Policy, etc)
New Students (First Time):
  1. Open the url, https://www.catamilacademy.org/cta/login.aspx and click the link ‘Student Registration’
  2. Click ‘No’ for the 2 questions or as appropriate and Click ‘Continue’
  3. Fill in the short form including Student/Parents Name and Click ‘Continue’
  4. Select 'NJ Thiruvalluvar Tamil School' on school site drop down list in the next screen
  5. Fill in all the details, marked *
  6. Select Other for public school city/name and fill in the box on the right side
  7. Click Submit button and follow the wizard (incl. agreeing/signing to Rules & Regulations, Waiver Policy, etc)
P.S. If registering second child, please follow 'Returning Parents' instructions
